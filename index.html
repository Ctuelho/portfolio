Tulio Duarte

Game Developer

I am a game developer and graphics programmer with more than 5 years of experience. Graduated as a Bachelor in Science and Technology by UFBA, I had the chance to learn Programming, Math, and Science, but I also studied Design and Arts.

During the college, I had the opportunity to participate in a scientific initiation where the theme was Art and Technology in Ecoarte. I was responsible for implementing interactive graphics applications, with Arduino and Processing, that compounded the piece of digital art. That was my first contact with computer graphics.

I also participated in a scientific initiation where we developed an experimental game to teach Physics to children. I had to implement some physics functions, such as buoyancy, and some AI such as the flocking algorithm. That was my first contact with Unity and C#, since then we live as a happy threesome.

I have worked on PC/Mobile games, 3D tools, and Augmented Reality applications and participated in many jams along those years. From Gameplay, AI, and UI, to visual effects, shaders, and optimizing rendering.

I am passionate about games and I love what I do, especially if it is Gameplay, AI or Game Design. If you need an all-rounded, experienced, many-skilled, and creative game developer, here I am!
