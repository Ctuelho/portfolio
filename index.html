Gomma

A game I made for Ludum Dare 50. My goals with this game were to create a sensation of solitude and slow-passing time. The player is alone in a desolate place and the only company they have is a giant slow-moving grub that will, eventually, pass, leaving the player alone again. The grub moves so slow that the player has time to cultivate plants to feed it, but also to stop it from moving while it is busy eating, delaying its departure. I was responsible for:

  • Game Design - I designed the whole game: world, characters, level, and the core mechanics.
  • I programmed the game.
  • Art - I made the sprites, animations, and visual effects.
  • I composed the title screen theme song.

Blank Knight

I prototyped an action roguelite with online content for mobile platforms.
